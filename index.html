2001: A Space Odessy

The Blue Danube Waltz

01 - A small space station floating alone in space.

02 - Camera pans out to reveal a planet.

03 - The planet is now the primary focus.

04 - a small space station orbiting the planet.

05 - the camera zooms out, and the space station falls away.

06 - a sunset over the horizon of a planet.

07 - The space station slowly comes into view over the horizon.

08 - The space station is now inview over the horizon.

09 - swiliring mass of blue and white.

10 - the swirling mass is revealed to be the planet below.

11 - Another space station floating in space.

12 - the moon gets revealed from behind the station.

13 - The moon is now the only thing in frame

14 - the camera cuts, the moon is replaced with a large space station.

15 - The camera pans into the large station.

16 - the camera continues panning in for an uncomfortable amount of time.

17 - The camera cuts back to the planet below.

18 - A sleek space ships comes into view.

19 - The planet below falls away.

20 - The camera cuts to the interior of the ship, it looks alot like the interior of an airplane.
